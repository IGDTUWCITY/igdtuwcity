Primary goal

Picture a place where words wield power, your voice ignites change, and your passion for words inspires others and sculpts the future. Welcome to B.H.A.V.: The Literary and Public Speaking Society of IGDTUW, where your passions are transformed into impactful expressions. Here, we believe in the transformative power of language, nurturing your ability to craft compelling narratives, deliver powerful speeches, and engage in thought-provoking discussions. Whether you're an aspiring author, a budding orator, or simply someone who loves the magic of words, B.H.A.V. is your platform to shine. Join us and discover a community that celebrates creativity, fosters growth, and empowers you to make a difference through the art of words.

Description

The Fantastic Four : Circles of B.H.A.V.
1. MUN Circle: The Diplomatic Discourse
The MUN Circle isn't just about Model United Nations; it's a realm where you sharpen your negotiation skills, broaden your geopolitical knowledge, and advocate for global solutions while striving for consensus in a thrilling simulation of the real UN.
Group Discussion Circle: The Collective Think-Tank
2. The Group Discussion Circle, where every opinion matters, and diverse perspectives collide and blend. It’s not just about speaking—it's about creating a symphony of ideas through respectful and stimulating dialogue.
3. Creative Writing Circle: The Chamber of Creativity
Unleash your imagination in the Creative Writing Circle, where your pen becomes the philosopher’s stone. Here, words transform into vivid landscapes, complex characters, and compelling narratives. Whether you’re crafting poetry that sings to the soul or prose that captures the heart, this circle is for you.
4. Debate Circle: The Stage of Spirited Speech
Step into the Debate Circle, where words become swords and logic is your shield. Whether you’re a novice finding your voice or a seasoned orator sharpening your skills, here, you will be challenged to think critically and argue convincingly.
We at B.H.A.V. celebrate the boundless power of your ideas and empower you to put them into words. Step into our world and let your voice inspire others as you leave an indelible mark with your opinions and arguments.
